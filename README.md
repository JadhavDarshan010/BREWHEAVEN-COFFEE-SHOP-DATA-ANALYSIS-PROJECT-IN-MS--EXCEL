## Project Insight
1. In 2025, BrewHeaven Coffee Shop earned a total annual revenue of ₹3.3 lakh and sold about 2,186 items.
2. January (₹31K) and June (₹30.5K) had the highest revenue, while April (₹22.1K) and November (₹21.3K) had the lowest.
3. Branch 2 earned the highest revenue (₹50.8K), followed by Branch 3 (₹48.7K) and Branch 6 (₹47.1K).  Branch 1 (₹31.7K) and Branch 5 (₹36.9K) earned the least revenue .
4. Uptown (₹67.4K) and Midtown (₹67K) regions ranked 1st and 2nd in total revenue. Downtown (₹52.7K) earned the lowest.
5.Top 3 products: 
      Cupcake – 158 sold, ₹21.7K revenue
      Smoothie – 137 sold, ₹16.8K revenue          
      Donut – 126 sold, ₹17.4K revenue
6. Bottom 3 products:
       Herbal Tea – 72 sold, ₹9.5K revenue
       Sandwich – 72 sold, ₹8.53K revenue
       Mocha – 43 sold, ₹5.17K revenue
7. Best category: Desserts (₹68.7K), followed by Tea (₹63K) and Cold Beverages (₹61K).  Lowest category: Coffee (₹53.1K).
8. The total annual profit was ₹1.8 lakh, with an average profit per sale of ₹315.
9.Tea Category gave the highest profit (₹48.9K), followed by Desserts Category (₹41.3K).  Snacks gave the lowest profit (₹25.8K).
10. Branches 4, 6, and 7 had the best profit-to-revenue ratio (15%), followed by Branches 1, 2, and 5 (14%) 
11. When discounts were given, about 1,500 items sold, earning ₹2.8 lakh revenue and ₹1.22 lakh profit.  Without discounts, only 642 items sold, making ₹94.4K revenue and ₹60K profit.  that means Sales, revenue, and profit doubled with discounts.
12. Customers preferred to pay by Cash (36%), followed by UPI (34%) and Card (30%).
13. Top 5 customers (by total purchase):CUST-1389 (₹4.2K), CUST-1819 (₹3.6K), CUST-1937 (₹3.5K), CUST-1341 (₹3.3K), and CUST-1501 (₹3.1K).
14.Midweek days (Tue & Wed) had more sales (₹97.9K) than weekends (Sat & Sun), which made ₹87.7K.  Mon, Thu & Fri together generated the highest revenue of ₹1.17 lakh.

## Conclusion :
1. BrewHeaven Coffee Shop earned ₹3.3 lakh revenue and ₹1.8 lakh profit in 2025 from 2,186 items sold.
2. January and June were the best-performing months, while April and November were the weakest.
3. Desserts and Tea were the top-selling categories, whereas Coffee and Snacks performed poorly.
4. Branch 2 and the Uptown region led in total revenue, while Branch 1 and Downtown were lowest.
5. Discounts and offers doubled sales, revenue, and profit, proving their strong impact.
6. Most customers preferred cash payments, followed by UPI and card methods.
7. Overall, the business had a profitable and consistent year with room for growth in specific areas.

## Next Plan :
1. Expand Discounts & Offers – Continue and promote offers like Buy 1 Get 1 to keep sales high.
2. Improve Low-Performing Branches – Focus on Branch 1 and 5 through promotions or better marketing.
3. Add New Dessert & Tea Items – Since these categories perform best, introduce new flavors or combos.
4. Boost Coffee & Snacks Sales – Redesign these products, adjust pricing, or create attractive combos.
5. Increase Digital Payments – Offer small rewards for customers using UPI or Card payments.
6. Special Campaigns in Weak Months – Run offers in April and November to improve performance.
7. Reward Loyal Customers – Give loyalty points or freebies to top buyers to maintain customer loyalty.
8. Use Data for Planning – Continue tracking sales trends to plan inventory, pricing, and marketing smartly.
